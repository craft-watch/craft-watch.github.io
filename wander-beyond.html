Maple Dream - Gluten-free, Lactose-free Maple Imperial Stout - x1

10.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats. Gluten free, tested bellow 20ppm - Vegan friendly

Maple Dream is a progression on the base ideas used in a previous gluten-free, lactose-free Imperial Sweet Stout that we brewed back in November 19, Leap. With some recipe adaptations and additions, this time we have created a Maple Imperial Stout which is still gluten-free and lactose-free, accessible to coeliacs, people with lactose and other gluten intolerance's, vegans and people who enjoy a good ol' Imperial Stout.

Raspberry Crash - Raspberry Imperial Stout - x1

10.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats

We do enjoy brewing fruited Imperial Stouts here at Wander Beyond. Raspberry Crash is a chewy Imperial Stout we re-fermented on 200g/L of raspberries before infusing it with vanilla and cacao nibs.

Flavours of rich chocolate and subtle roasted malt notes are combined with the ripeness from the raspberries to create this deviously delicious stout.

Peak - Pale Ale - x1

3.8% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats, Wheat

This beer normally doesn't travel much further than Manchester as it is primarily brewed for cask. However we're excited to release this can version for you to enjoy both in Manchester or further afield.

Ramble - Session Pale Ale - x1

4.2% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats, Wheat

Ramble is a light sessionable pale ale coming in at 4.2% ABV. A light malt character balanced with a subtle dry hop ensures the sessionable drinkability of this pale ale.

Lutra Lutra - Pale Ale - x1

5.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats, Wheat

Lutra Lutra is a straight forward easy drinking pale ale. A nice simple malt based combined with a modest level of hops to create a no fuss, quaffable, slightly hazy, pale ale.

Pursuit - Gluten-free, Lactose-free Imperial Milkshake IPA - x2

10.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats, Wheat. Gluten free, tested bellow 20ppm

Pursuit is a gluten and lactose free version of one of our favourite beers to brew, Illicium. Sleepy Hollow - IPA - x4

7.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Wheat, Oats

For an IPA, Sleepy Hollow has a medium to thick body, which carries forward citrus flavours of sweet orange and pink grapefruit. These are backed up with tropical notes of mango and pineapple, with a slight dankness and a moderately bitter finish.

Mixed 12 pack

Case contains 12x 440ml cans:

Curious Creek - Berliner Weisse with raspberries and cherries - x1

ABV: 3.2% - Size: 440ml - Allergens: Wheat, Malted Barley - Vegan friendly

Curious Creek was our first Berliner Weisse and is fruited with 125g/l of raspberries & cherries. It's bright, tart and crisp, and at only 3.2% this sour beer is distinctive, refreshing and packs a punch.

Captivating Creek - Double Berliner Weisse with raspberries and cherries - x2

ABV: 6.4% - Size: 440ml - Allergens: Wheat, Malted Barley - Vegan friendly

Captivating Creek has double the fruit and double the ABV of it's little sibling Curious Creek.

With a slightly thicker mouthfeel and a jammier fruit profile, Captivating Creek is similar enough to remind us of the Curious Creek we love but a whole new beer in itself!

Colossal Creek - Triple Berliner Weisse with raspberries and cherries - x2

ABV: 9.6% - Size: 440ml - Allergens: Wheat, Malted Barley - Vegan friendly

Colossal creek is an experimental Triple Berliner Weisse. We took our 'Curious Creek' recipe and tripled everything. Three times the grist, three times the ABV and three times the amount of fruit. Re-fermented on nearly 3/4 Tonne of Raspberries and cherries this beer is fruity, tart and full on.

Coconut Crater - Imperial Milk Stout with coconut, cacao and vanilla - x1

12% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats, Lactose - Vegetarian friendly

We brewed a big Imperial Milk Stout and infused it with a healthy dose of toasted coconut and a pleasant amount of cacao and vanilla for a thick and decadent treat.

Dolis - Imperial Stout with Peruvian cacao and vanilla - x1

11.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats- Vegan friendly

Dolis is brewed with a combination of specialty malts, Peruvian cacao nibs and vanilla to create a rich and luxurious imperial stout. Strong flavours of dark chocolate and roasted malt characteristics contrast with sweeter notes of vanilla.

The name and artwork for Dolis is inspired by our designer Tina's favourite moorland crag and one of it's classic f6c boulder problems. Tina enjoyed this climb so much at the weekend, she came to work on Monday and put it on a can! This technical climb has a mix of strong and flexy moves but she says it's the beautiful isolated location that gives it its charm.

Octopod - Imperial Milkshake IPA with mango and passionfruit - x1

12% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats, Lactose - Vegetarian friendly

Octopod is a hyper-viscous Imperial Milkshake IPA with a good helping of lactose and vanilla. Re-fermented on over half a tonne (more than 300g/l) of alphonso mangoes and passionfruit. Generously dry hopped post-fermentation with both Citra and Mosaic T90 hops.

Sleepy Hollow - IPA - x3
