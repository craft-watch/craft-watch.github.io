Winter Gold in 2 or 4 Pint Cartons

Our fantastic Winter Gold was so incredibly popular last year that we've given in to demand and brewed it again.

Brewed with hearty oats and rich, biscuity malts, this elegant golden ale is silky and sumptuous and the perfect addition to any fireside.

A charming medley of British hops (including Bullion, Challenger, Admiral, and Goldings) combine to give this beer it's festive glow; imparting warm, zesty undertones, spicy cedar notes and hints of festive redcurrant.

At 4.3% ABV it's highly sessionable and ideal for cosying up with throughout winter.
