Lichtenhainer

Lichtenhainer is a 3.8% smoky and lightly sour pale beer from the Thuringia area of Germany, an obscure and esoteric beer-style that almost completely disappeared during the 20th century.

It has a gentle smoky flavour imparted by beechwood smoked malted barley, like a distant campfire. A lactic tang of acidity cuts through the bready, cereal-like body to add balance and drinkability in the relative absence of any hop bitterness. The carbonation is lively and brings a crisp & refreshing carbonic bite.

The perfect light beer for when the sharp summer sun fades into the smoky comfort of Autumn.

Dead Wax London Porter

A beer to fit tightly within our ethos at Orbit Beers, paying homage to European brewing heritage and traditions. Named after the runoff area on vinyl records, with a nod to Seven (named for the 7” single) our Double Brown Stout which we have brewed in previous winters at Orbit.

Dead Wax is our take on a classic London Porter. Based on the late nineteenth-century interpretation of the style. Complex rich malt, with aromas of chocolate, coffee, caramel and malty biscuit. More chocolate, coffee and black treacle on the palate. Rich full body, robust bitterness and a subtle earthy/lemony hop character combine to create a distinctively dry and drinkable dark ruby coloured beer.

Sticke Alt

Inspired by the legend of the Sticke Fest and Sticke Alt. Twice annually, a special strong version of the Dusseldorf Altbier is brewed by three breweries in the Dusseldorf Altstadt (old town). The beer is put on the bar (literally, wooden barrels are hoisted up on to the bar and tapped) twice a year and enjoyed only by locals lucky enough to be in the know regarding the availability of this special release.

Our homage is coppery red with notes of toffee, raisin, bread, spice and herbs. A full body, aggressive bitterness and long, clean finish thanks to the lagering time. A particular nod to Zum Uerige Sticke Altbier, traditionally drunk on the third Tuesday in January and October, as well as ‘Neu’ Orbit’s original strength Altbier from our back catalogue.
