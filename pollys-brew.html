Bonus Tracks

Range: Augment

Style: India Pale Ale

ABV: 6.4%

We’ll always maintain that a Citra and Mosaic base dry hop is unbeatable, but where to go from there? We’re already massive Sabro converts, so it was a given that we’d be adding this, but why not throw some Bru-1 into the mix – a hop varietal we’ve as yet not used at Polly’s. Named for an “ism” coined from the brewery when we discovered all these new varietals now available, we’ve supercharged this beer with a dry hop of 40kg, resulting in freshly cut pineapple aroma and flavours from Bru-1 complimenting the pina colada notes of Sabro, and accentuating the tropical fruit notes of Citra and Mosaic in an IPA that is mega juicy, mega hazy, and mega Polly’s.

Cahb

Citra + Galaxy DIPA

You want the juice? We brought the juice! Sometimes you just need to take a look at the hops you have available in your store and go “I feel like making something really special”. Citra and Galaxy – need we say more. Pure orange sherbert on the nose, with fresh squeezed mango juice to boot. On the palate, Galaxy takes the stage and shows exactly why it’s one of the most sought-after hop varietals on the planet. Passionfruit, peach, and citrus notes a-plenty, paired up with huge mouthfeel thanks to the generous helping of oats in the mash. All wrapped up in a healthy lashing of LA3 for maximum suspension, this is exactly where we want our DIPAs to be.

Informal Edits

IPA

IPA – the style without which we wouldn’t be here today. We are famous (or infamous, your call!) for our passion for paying homage to all things old school here at Polly’s – tipping a glass to the OG craft beer heroes. With Informal Edits, we wanted to smash together the classic old school bite of a west coast IPA with the soft, pillowy juice of a NEIPA for a hybridised IPA that carries the qualities of both. A heavy heavy 40kg dry hop charge of Citra and Columbus pairs up with a 10 minute whirlpool addition of even more Citra for an IPA that has equal parts soft and delicate notes, with super soft juicy notes to boot, with maximum drinkability in mind.
