Lawless Lager

Just added

Lawless Lager, our maverick brew follows the 500- year-old Bavarian brewing law to achieve traditional purity using only four ingredients. We then added an idiosyncratic twist of our own, creating a blend of Hallertau Northern Brewer, Eldorado and Styrian Goldings hops. With Pilsner Malt, Wheat Malt, Caragold, lager yeast and our own mineral rich local water. Pure Cider

Just added

Pure Cider is created in partnership with Celtic Marches, artisan producers of premium quality cider. Exclusively sourced from the Frome Valley, Pure Cider is a medium, well rounded Herefordshire cider made from 100% Herefordshire apples. Every apple is picked from orchards within a 1.5 Mile radius of the Celtic Marches farm. They’re then pressed on the very same day. Pure Cider is authenticated by a protected geographical indicator (PGI) which certifies it is a genuine Herefordshire product.

An easy drinking 4.6% cider with a mellow flavour and slight tart finish. The cider is allowed to mature for 3-4 months to develop the very best flavour profile. Locally sourced mineral water is used in the fermentation process, creating a clean, crisp and easy drinking cider.

Pork Loin, Black Pudding, Sweetheart Cabbage & Smoked Pancetta, Pommes Anna & Plum Jus
