6 × RODEO Pale Ale • 4.6%

One of our first pals. Rodeo is a light straw colour with a gentle haze. Citrus and grapefruit aromas and flavours come from Citra and Cascade, giving a balanced floral tone. Low bitterness and a soft body. Real refreshing!

6 × RAFIKI Session IPA • 4.3%

Our low-strength IPA, and a family fave. Tropical notes of mango, passionfruit and guava come from Mosaic and Ekuanot. Smooth bitterness and a sweet biscuity malt flavour.

6 × ISOLATION IPA Number 3 • 5.4%

This is the third Isolation IPA we've brewed, and this one's been hopped with Amarillo, Centennial and Chinook. Really nicely balanced! We're getting orange, grapefruit and a resinous pine, pleasant late bitterness too. Delish! All proceeds from the sale of Isolation IPA will be donated to the National Emergencies Trust.

Toto is a 5.3% American Pale hopped with Idaho 7, Simcoe and Ekuanot and fermented with London Ale III. Real haps with this one! Full-bodied, lovely soft bitterness. Tropical, citrus and pine flaves. Limited run!

IPA

The National Emergencies Trust (NET), in partnership with the British Red Cross, have launched an appeal to raise funds to support communities made most vulnerable by the coronavirus outbreak. The NET works with 46 local community groups across the UK, all of which are grassroots charities best placed to help people affected.

ALL PROCEEDS FROM THE SALE OF ISOLATION IPA WILL BE DONATED TO THE NET.

This is the third Isolation IPA we've brewed, and this one's been hopped with Amarillo, Centennial and Chinook. Really nicely balanced! We're getting orange, grapefruit and a resinous pine, pleasant late bitterness too. Delish! For the malt bill, we used Golden Promise, Wheat, Dextrin, Golden Naked Oats and Caramalt. Cheers!
