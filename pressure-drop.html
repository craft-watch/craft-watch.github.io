Dreamlife

New England Pale Ale

NEW !!!

New England Pale 4.6%

Lie down in the green grass, look up into the blue skies and contemplate life, dreams and everything in between. Dreamlife is a soft & hazy pale ale, made for easy drinking and easy dreaming.

Made with hops from all corners of the globe, it’s a sessionable New England pale ale brewed with Nelson Sauvin from New Zealand, Amarillo from the U.S. and Mandarina Bavaria from Germany.

It has distinct citrus notes of orange and grapefruit imparted by the Amarillo and Manadarina hops, while the Huell Melon gives the beer a green fruit & melon aroma which carries through into the palate.

Dream a little dream of Dreamlife.

Pale Fire

Mosaic & Amarillo Pale

4.8% Amarillo & Mosaic Pale Ale 440ml Can

Pale Fire.

We’ve come a long long way together.

If there is one beer that we think of when our minds drift to what Pressure Drop means, it is Pale Fire. Brewed every week since our earliest days, it is our shining, hazy gold, North Star of a beer.

Pale Fire is the definition of the Pressure Drop mantra - “We brew what we would like to drink”. Sessionable, with tropical aromas from the Mosaic and Amarillo hops, it is a juicy pale with a balanced bitter finish.

When we first began experimenting with canning here at Pressure Drop, the end goal was always going to be Pale Fire in cans. Spending time with pals, opening a six pack of fresh and crisp Pale Fire, enjoying its easy drinking ways. That has been the dream, now it is the reality.

Find your nearest sun drenched swimming pool, hike in the gentle shade of a pine forest, or, failing that, sit in the hazy summer heat of a city park. Crack open a can, and let the world become a better place.

Great Heights

Unfiltered Pilsner

4.8% Unfiltered Pilsner

A brewer’s life is a busy one. It’s not always easy to get a break. However, sometimes a break can result in some great brewing.

Great Heights is a pilsner that is lagered in tank for six weeks, to achieve maximum crispy freshness. Mittelfruh & Perle hops for a classic light bitterness, Great Heights is an uncomplicated delight. A great pilsner is a true and simple joy, and aren’t those the best kind of joys you can have?
