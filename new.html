IPA

New to craft.watch

Mylar

The part of Wales we’re based is an incredible mish-mash of culture, and it’s reflected best in the dialect – part-Welsh, part-Liverpudlian, and by proxy, part-Irish. This dictates a lot of the day to day sayings in the brewery, and when we were sat in a meeting talking about various recipes submitted by the team, someone shouted up “That’s mine, la”. The beer itself is textbook Polly’s – high juice, low bitterness, accentuated by our generous use of Citra, Mosaic, and Sabro in the dry hop. Expect a swagbag of honeydew melon, mango, tangerine, and pina colada flavours abound from the Sabro. A beer that is an homage to the wonderfully bizarre part of the world we grew up.

Black Sands - Imperial Stout with cinnamon and coconut - x1

10.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats - Vegitarian friendly

Blacks Sands is a pitch black Imperial Stout with cinnamon and coconut. It delivers initial flavours of cinnamon which then blends with the coconut, developing into a candied sweetness from the lactose and vanilla, with underlying notes of chocolate. 

Maple Dream - Gluten-free, Lactose-free Maple Imperial Stout - x1

10.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats. Gluten free, tested bellow 20ppm - Vegan friendly

Maple Dream is a progression on the base ideas used in a previous gluten-free, lactose-free Imperial Sweet Stout that we brewed back in November 19, Leap. With some recipe adaptations and additions, this time we have created a Maple Imperial Stout which is still gluten-free and lactose-free, accessible to coeliacs, people with lactose and other gluten intolerance's, vegans and people who enjoy a good ol' Imperial Stout.

Raspberry Crash - Raspberry Imperial Stout - x1

10.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats - Vegan friendly

We do enjoy brewing fruited Imperial Stouts here at Wander Beyond. Raspberry Crash is a chewy Imperial Stout we re-fermented on 200g/L of raspberries before infusing it with vanilla and cacao nibs.

Flavours of rich chocolate and subtle roasted malt notes are combined with the ripeness from the raspberries to create this deviously delicious stout.

Mini City Of Gold - Table Beer - x1

3.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats, Wheat, Lactose - Vegetarian friendly

Mini City of Gold is a highly sessionable version of our 5% milkshake IPA 'City Of Gold'. A creamy 3% base beer is charged with El Dorado and Azacca hops before being infused with vanilla. A beer full of flavour and low on alcohol, perfect for the summer.

Gasp - Milkshake IPA with Peach and Apricot - x1

7.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats, Wheat, Lactose - Vegetarian friendly

The name Gasp is a nod back to a similar brew of ours that you may remember, Gulp.

Gasp is a deliciously fruited Milkshake IPA. Fermented on 200g/l of peaches and apricots. The sweetness from the fruits and lactose is balanced against a backdrop of citrus notes, provided by the Chinook dry hop. This is a refreshing yet moreish little number.

Octo-pie - Passionfruit Imperial Milkshake IPA - x1

9.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats, Wheat, Lactose - Vegetarian friendly

Octo-pie is an Imperial passion fruit Milkshake IPA. Overloaded with half a tonne of tart and tangy passion fruit and infused with vanilla and creamy lactose, this is a refreshingly sharp yet indulgent dessert-like treat.

Sleepy Hollow - IPA - x4

7.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Wheat, Oats - Vegan friendly

For an IPA, Sleepy Hollow has a medium to thick body, which carries forward citrus flavours of sweet orange and pink grapefruit. These are backed up with tropical notes of mango and pineapple, with a slight dankness and a moderately bitter finish.

As for the artwork, the can pictures the hops Citra, Mosaic and Simcoe, climbing an enchanting boulder problem in the North Yorkshire Moors called 'Sleepy Hollow'. The picturesque boulder hides in the forest with a tree emerging from within it. The iconic line traverses a sloping block with big slaps and technical footwork (for those who climb, it's pretty tough and graded 7c+ from a sit).

Pursuit - Gluten-free, Lactose-free Imperial Milkshake IPA - x2

10.0% ABV - 440ml - Allergens: Barley, Oats, Wheat. Gluten free, tested bellow 20ppm - Vegan friendly

Pursuit is a gluten and lactose free version of one of our favourite beers to brew, Illicium. We believe we have successfully managed to create a doppelganger in Pursuit, which despite the lack of lactose still delivers on the sweet smoothness of it’s older sibling. Balanced with the punchy fruit flavours of the Citra and Mosaic hops to create a sweet dessert style Imperial Milkshake IPA, Pursuit is accessible to coeliacs, people with other gluten and lactose intolerance's and vegans.

Berliner Weisse
