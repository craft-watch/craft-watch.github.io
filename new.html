Northern Monk
Ofs006

British Summer Fruit Punch Sour

NEW !!!

4.3% ABV // 1.048 OG // 9 EBC // 15 IBU // 440ML

INGREDIENTS: Water, Malted BARLEY (GLUTEN), Malted WHEAT (GLUTEN), OATS (GLUTEN), Strawberry, Orange, Red Grape, Mint, Cucumber, Orange Peel, Lemon Peel, Hops, Yeast

We wanted to recreate the experience of sitting in the sun, drinking that world-renowned English summer punch, made to the classic spec. A complex mix of adjuncts take centre stage: strawberry, red grape, mint, orange, cucumber, orange peel and lemon peel. To create a beer as similar to its inspiration as possible, we used Vic Secret to bring a gentle strawberry note in the kettle, and added a whole load of different fruits and peels to bring flavour, with a gentle sourness letting the rest of the ingredients sing.
