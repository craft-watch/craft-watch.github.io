Limited Edition - Scrubs Charity Lager Beer

Matured for 100 days

Just added

All profit from this beer is for The Care Workers Charity who provide essential support to carers with one-off crisis grants. Care workers look after our society’s most vulnerable individuals, often for little reward. They spend their careers assisting others, but when circumstances change, sometimes it is the carers who need some help. In recent times, their service to society has never been more greatly appreciated. Care workers, thank you… this beer is for you!

This limited edition of our flagship lager has been maturing throughout lockdown, 100 days in total, and the longest we have ever matured a lager for. Over that time the flavour has developed and become more refined, a perfect way to toast our nation’s heroes and help protect them too.

Thank you to our care workers from the Innis & Gunn family.

All suppliers involved have provided their goods at cost price to increase the donations – Robinson’s Brewery, Multi-Colour Corporation, Russell's Logistics.

No discounts are applicable on this charity beer.

© Innis & Gunn

Rye Whiskey Cask

Ruby Scotch Ale

Just added

For centuries, Canada and Scotland have enjoyed a very special bond; a shared history built on the progressive values of inclusivity, tolerance and humility. Ocean-crossing ties that bind.

In celebration of Canada Day 2020 we have created a beer beyond the combination of grain, hops, yeast and water; one that instead represents a meeting of two countries, who’s rich and bountiful resources have helped to shape world-class spirits enjoyed far beyond their shores.

Matured in Canada’s finest rye whiskey casks, this beer embodies our unique connection in a bottle. The flavour from the oak barrels transforms the beer, creating a perfectly balanced blend of rye whiskey's spicy, sweetness and Scottish malts’ rich, earthiness.

Two countries intertwined; one outstanding beer that’s as irresistible as the friendship that underpins it.
