The Altar Supreme

Range: Augment

Style: India Pale Ale

ABV: 5.9%

We’ve said it once and we’ll say it again, we love paying homage to the OGs of brewing – the brewers who produced the beers without which we wouldn’t be here today. This rings true for wanting to pay homage to the classic IPAs – slightly weaker in strength than our usual 6%+ fare, but retaining all that incredible flavour profile, body, and mouthfeel of what we do best. The Altar Supreme is our take on those classic IPAs – coming in at a more reasonable 5.9% but supercharged with 30kg of El Dorado and Vic Secret for all those new school notes. A beer that brings all those juicy, candied orange, stone fruit, and mango notes we’re so famous for to the party initially, but backed up with a delicious resinous bitterness for complete balance.

The New Renaissance

Double IPA

NEW !!!

The New Renaissance

Range: Augment

Style: Double India Pale Ale

ABV: 8.0%

We’re proud to be part of what is a constantly growing and evolving scene of breweries and drinkers alike. Beer as it is today is a world apart from when we were taking our first tentative steps into being legally able to drink, and an off hand phrase made in the office about the brewing industry entering into a “New Renaissance” birthed the name of this DIPA that embraces all the techniques we ourselves have learned in such a short space of time. Utilizing Azacca, Mosaic, El Dorado and Simcoe in our pyramid dry hopping technique, with a ten minute whirlpool addition of Vic Secret and El Dorado, all wrapped up in our bespoke, custom designed LA3 yeast strain, this beer shouts for everything modern when it comes to UK brewing.
