Northern Monk
26.02 Sarah Harris

Culinary Adventures: Scarborough // Double Cone Ice Cream Pale

NEW !!!

8.2% ABV // 1.090 OG // 10 EBC // 20 IBU // 440ML

INGREDIENTS: Malted BARLEY (GLUTEN), OATS (GLUTEN), LACTOSE (MILK), Strawberry, Hops, Chocolate, Vanilla, Yeast

As we enter summer, our patrons series tour with Sarah Harris takes us to the Scarborough coast. The sun is in the sky, the fish and chips were fresh and salty and all that is left is to complete the perfect day is to head down to the beach, picking up a double cone ice cream (with flake) on the way! This double ice cream pale is designed to replicate that taste of the seaside in delicious beer form. We built the beer up over a base of silky flaked oats and mouthfeel-building chit malt as part of an extra pale malt grist. To bring out the creaminess we craved, we combined lactose milk sugar and Madagascan vanilla to bring out the most classic of ice cream flavours. For our second scoop, we went with 100g/l of strawberry puree during fermentation and finished off with the all-important flake, with a late addition of milk chocolate.

Our most exciting mixed case yet. Sample our two new mixed fermentation beers; Hinterland and Narrow Sea - the first releases from our Barrel Store. You’ll also get three cans each of our two best sellers, Kaleidoscope and Sundance, making this the perfect summer beer selection.

A fiercely flavoursome IPA, Hinterland explodes with vibrant aromas of ripe pineapple and fresh, juicy oranges. Softer fruits then linger into a crisp, satisfying finish. Added layers of complexity emerge with time, the result of a careful blend of Brettanomyces and traditional brewer’s yeasts.

Narrow Sea is our very English take on a Belgian beer style. Brewed with a combination of traditional Yorkshire yeast and a British heritage Brettanomyces strain, along with native hops and malts, this light English Saison walks firmly on the dry side of life. Savour the alluring crisp aroma with subtle hints of orchard fruits and elderflower.

Kaleidoscope combines three different hops to create a seasonally evolving flavour profile. Whilst the hops may change, our intention remains the same - to create a beer that is harmonious, bright and refreshing.

Bursting with juicy tropical fruit flavours such as mango, pineapple and papaya and layered with berry and citrus notes, Sundance combines the finest US and UK hops to create a crisp, complex and highly refreshing IPA.

INGREDIENTS: Water, Malted BARLEY (GLUTEN), Malted WHEAT (GLUTEN), OATS (GLUTEN), Dextrose, Hops, Yeast

An IPA that continues our experiments with Kveik yeast. For this beer we aimed for pronounced orange notes, an ultra-hazy appearance and a thick mouthfeel. Loaded with oats and utilising a soft, chloride-heavy water profile, we dual-hopped with Strata and Citra at every stage. This IPA has heavy notes of citrus and passion fruit, finishing with a touch of pepper and lime zest.

Imperial Milkshake IPA with mango and passionfruit

NEW !!!

Octopod - Imperial Milkshake IPA with mango and passionfruit

Octopod is a hyper-viscous Imperial Milkshake IPA with a good helping of lactose and vanilla. Re-fermented on over half a tonne (more than 300g/l) of alphonso mangoes and passionfruit. Generously dry hopped post-fermentation with both Citra and Mosaic T90 hops.

We have decided to release this batch super fresh to give you an opportunity to try Octopod as fresh as possible and in all it’s hoppy glory, before the ‘hop burn’ and some of the more volatile hop characteristics mellow out, leaving the Octopod you know and love.

We are excited to hear your folks opinions on whether you prefer Octopod super fresh with the more dominant hop profile or if you enjoy it more once it has softened and all the big punchy flavours have had a chance to meld together. Get back to us and let us know what you think.
