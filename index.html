Astro Gary // Rainbow Astro Belt Sour IPA

NEW !!!

6.5% ABV // 1.0735 OG // 6 EBC // 15 IBU // 440ML

INGREDIENTS: Malted BARLEY (GLUTEN), OATS (GLUTEN), LACTOSE (MILK), Mango Puree, Raspberry Puree, Orange Juice Concentrate, Lime Juice Concentrate, Hops, Yeast

FOR THIS NEW PATRONS PROJECT SERIES WITH LEIMAI LEMAOW, WE’RE GOING

TO BE TAKING A TRIP TO THE SWEET SIDE. LEIMAI IS A SELF-PROCLAIMED CANDY NUT, SO WE’LL BE TAKING OUR INSPIRATION FOR THE SERIES FROM HER PERSONAL FAVOURITE SWEETS, TO CREATE TASTES OF CHILDHOOD IN LIQUID FORM.

First up, a sour IPA inspired by the colours and flavours of rainbow Astro Belts. We started with a simple hazy IPA base of pale barley malt and plenty of oats, backed up with lactose to round out the mouthfeel. We whirlpool hopped with Citra BBC and then fermented with our house IPA yeast strain.

We selected fruits to represent the colours of the candy that we felt worked well together. Leading with the ripe fruit flavours of mango puree and orange juice, we followed up with plenty of tangy raspberry puree, and finally, a touch of zingy lime juice to give a sour accent to the finish.

Hefe-weisse

NEW !!!

What it is, is what it is, maybe not so much what you think it is.

Weissbier is a pillar of modern beer. This classic German beer category originated in the 15th century Bavaria but is now celebrated the world over. Hefeweissbier refers specifically to an unfiltered wheat beer that's hazy in appearance due to the presence of yeast. Known for distinctive flavours of banana and clove, it's creamy and smooth while remaining effortlessly drinkable and beautifully refreshing.
