NEW !!!Mixed case

THE BAR-BEER-QUE 12 x 440ml Pack

This mixed case contains a selection of sunnytime classics to suit all tastes (and go with whatever is on the grill). Beers included:

3 x FLIPSIDE – DDH PALE ALE (NELSON AMARILLO CITRA) – 5.2% ABV

Flipside is a DDH Pale Ale hopped with Nelson, Amarillo and Citra. Tangy white grape notes offset the sharp citrus and sweet peach flavours in this extra pale, extra balanced, hazy crusher.

3 x PARK VIEW – SUMMER WITBIER (MANDARINA BAVARIA) – 4.5% ABV

Park View, a super-quenching Summer Wit hopped with Mandarina Bavaria. Delicate notes of clementine and lemon in this ultra-pale, spritzy and aromatic Witbier are perfectly paired with sitting on a blanket.

3 x FAMOUS BLUE CAGOULE – PALE ALE (NELSON CITRA) – 4.0% ABV

Famous Blue Cagoule is our highly practical Pale Ale for uncertain times and unpredictable weather. Citra and Nelson zip across the palate with those upbeat notes of grape and mango, and keep the finish nice and dry.

3 x BARLEYPOP – LITE LAGER – 4.4% ABV

POP-POP! Barleypop is our new 4.4% Lite Lager. Putting some polish on the well-worn everyday light lager, this is an extremely sunny beer designed for drinking with grass between your toes.
