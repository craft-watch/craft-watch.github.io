Redchurch Shoreditch Blonde

NEW !!!

It’s time to bring some Belgian inspired beer to the hipster hub of East London and beyond. This is unlike no other beer that you would have tasted by a British brewery. An individual within its class…a standout. So very Shoreditch. A fantastic beer for those looking to expand their beer palate. If you are partial to white wine this could be the beer for you. Pairs great with seafood dishes, cheese boards and a nice bit of crusty bread…we like grabbing a few of these for a day spent on the picnic blanket!

Saison yeast is the star of the show and pairs with European hops to give a fruit and banana aroma. Bitter but fruity, Extra pale malt and wheat malt give a sweetness and full body.

Redchurch Paradise IPA

NEW !!!

Brewed with you in mind, this session IPA is there for those moments you want an all-day drinking beer or perhaps a beer to enjoy at lunch time before heading back to the office. The name stems from Paradise Row in Bethnal Green which boasts a rich 340-year history and today is a social hub for those calling East London home. A cosmopolitan oasis that creates a little piece of East London Paradise….and that’s why we love it!

The moderate alcohol content is supported by a strong malt base with added oats for a fuller body. The bouquet is explosive with zesty fresh lime, tropical fruits and citrus making this beer fresh, easy-drinking and complex in equal measure.
