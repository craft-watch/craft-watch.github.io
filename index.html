Purity
Lawless Lager

NEW !!!

Lawless Lager, our maverick brew follows the 500- year-old Bavarian brewing law to achieve traditional purity using only four ingredients. We then added an idiosyncratic twist of our own, creating a blend of Hallertau Northern Brewer, Eldorado and Styrian Goldings hops. With Pilsner Malt, Wheat Malt, Caragold, lager yeast and our own mineral rich local water. Coolly lagered for 40 days – oh, and we slipped in a dash of hops, whilst no one was looking – for a sharp, citrusy kick.

Lawless Lager is best served with pot roasted sustainable cod (such as Skrei), chickpeas and chorizo.
