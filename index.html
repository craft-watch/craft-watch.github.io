Range: Augment

Our staff designed DDH Pale series is certainly gaining some serious traction now, and we’re rolling onto another one in rapid time before we take a short break, with the arrival of Reality Dysfunction – designed entirely by our assistant brewer Matt.

Expressing his love for our lactose-tinged output, Matt brought the mash temperature right down to ensure the beer wasn’t overly sweet, and hit the dry hop up with a bumper dry hop charge of Enigma for a symphony of flavours that dance throughout.

NEW !!!

DDH Oh My…

Range: Augment

Style: Double India Pale Ale

ABV: 8.5%

The original version of Oh My… turned out to have something in the way of a cult following when we released it back in April. Our collaboration with Chamonix-based Big Mountain Brewing Co utilised everybody’s favourite hype hop Sabro for the first time and set off an obsession which continues to this day, thanks to those super complex coconut, pina colada flavours, paired up with our old faithful Citra for some citrus balance. Fast forward to the present day, with a tonne of both Sabro and Citra in our cold store, and with the blessing of our French pals, we decided to scale up Oh My… with the Polly’s DIPA treatment. An insane dry hop charge of 50kg means those juicy pina colada notes are amplified to the max for a DIPA that drinks well below its ABV.
