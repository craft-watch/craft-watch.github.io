Polly's Brew
A Cozy Decomposer

DDH IPA

NEW !!!

A Cozy Decomposure

Range: Augment

Style: DDH India Pale Ale

ABV: 6.6%

Azacca is a big favourite here at Polly’s HQ and for good reason. Named for the Haitian god of agriculture, and bred specifically to accentuate the intense sweet, tropical notes it imparts, we find this varietal grossly underused and underrated in a sea of more popular hype hops. Letting it do its thing in a single hop showcase seemed the most fitting tribute for this awesome hop, and in a “well, why not” move we decided to up the dry-hop bill to DIPA levels, with an insane 40kg added during fermentation.

Wrapped up in a hearty lashing of LA3 for maximum suspension, the result is a thick, juicy IPA, bursting with tonnes of mango, stone fruit, and pineapple notes, before levelling off with a healthy tickling of bitterness on the end for ultimate balance.

Our favourite DIPA to brew, now in TIPA form, Rosa blooms with a bouquet of everything dank and juicy.

The New Renaissance

Range: Augment

Style: Double India Pale Ale

ABV: 8.0%

We’re proud to be part of what is a constantly growing and evolving scene of breweries and drinkers alike. Beer as it is today is a world apart from when we were taking our first tentative steps into being legally able to drink, and an off hand phrase made in the office about the brewing industry entering into a “New Renaissance” birthed the name of this DIPA that embraces all the techniques we ourselves have learned in such a short space of time. Utilizing Azacca, Mosaic, El Dorado and Simcoe in our pyramid dry hopping technique, with a ten minute whirlpool addition of Vic Secret and El Dorado, all wrapped up in our bespoke, custom designed LA3 yeast strain, this beer shouts for everything modern when it comes to UK brewing.
