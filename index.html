Lichtenhainer

NEW !!!

Lichtenhainer is a 3.8% smoky and lightly sour pale beer from the Saxony area of Germany, an obscure and esoteric beer-style that almost completely disappeared during the 20th century.

It has a gentle smoky flavour imparted by beechwood smoked malted barley, like a distant campfire. A lactic tang of acidity cuts through the bready, cereal-like body to add balance and drinkability in the relative absence of any hop bitterness. The carbonation is lively and brings a crisp & refreshing carbonic bite.

The perfect light beer for when the sharp summer sun fades in to the smoky comfort of Autumn.

Odyssey 009

Barrel-Aged Sour Stout Blend

NEW !!!

Odyssey 009 is a blend of sherry and bourbon barrel-aged imperial stouts and red wine barrel-aged saison, inoculated with brett and lactic-acid. Inspired by Project Barista, the beer is a complex blend of flavours found in your coffee cup - fruity acidity, roasted chocolate, nuttiness and a rounded caramel and toffee finish. A small addition of Brazilian coffee from Anvil in Basingstoke accentuates the sweetness.

Sleepy Hollow - IPA x12

For an IPA, Sleepy Hollow has a medium to thick body, which carries forward citrus flavours of sweet orange and pink grapefruit. These are backed up with tropical notes of mango and pineapple, with a slight dankness and a moderately bitter finish.
