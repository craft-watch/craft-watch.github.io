With this year's hop harvest in full swing, we looked to our fantastic local community to help us create an incredibly special beer marking the occasion.

THE TOP TWELVE 12-Pack [+ FREE DELIVERY]

NEW !!!Mixed case

THE TOP TWELVE 12 x 440ml Pack

A highly limited, always-evolving selection, packed with all of our latest and greatest. Each case of 12 cans will consist of 12 different beers. Cases cannot be customised. Beers included:

VELVET SPOTLIGHT – DIPA (SABRO CRYO CITRA CRYO SIMCOE) – 8.2% ABV

Velvet Spotlight is our latest DIPA, packing in spectacular Sabro, Cryo Citra and Cryo Simcoe. Big, sticky mango and pineapple vibes take centre stage, softened with a little coconut and lime.

ISLANDS – NEIPA (GALAXY MOSAIC) – 7.3% ABV

Islands is a 7.3% NEIPA showcasing Galaxy and Mosaic. This beer is a full-spectrum tropical assault. Massive mangoes, colossal pineapples, titanically dank. Galaxy and Mosaic fighting for your attention, and the only winner is you.

FREEDOM VORTEX – WEST COAST IPA (CITRA CENTENNIAL COLUMBUS) – 7.2% ABV

Citra, Centennial and Columbus (name a more iconic trio we’ll wait) join forces for the Westie comeback tour. West Coast IPA just like mama used to make: blood orange, pine and grapefruit for days, with IBUs and whatnot.

PANIC KIT – KVEIK NEIPA (AZACCA CASCADE CITRA) – 7.0% ABV

Azacca? ✅ Cascade? ✅ Citra? ✅ Kveik Yeast? ✅ Our new Panic Kit NEIPA has everything you need. Bursting with danksplosions of grapefruit, orange and pineapple, delivered by the power of Kveik. Panic over!

CABIN FEVER – NEIPA (AZACCA TOPAZ CENTENNIAL) – 6.6% ABV

Once you’re shacked up with Azacca, Topaz and Centennial, you’ll never want to leave. Intense tropical fruit and citrus vibes climb the walls of this new 6.6% NEIPA.

MEANWHILE UPTOWN – NEIPA (CRYO AZACCA CRYO CITRA STRATA) – 6.7% ABV

Our first beer with Strata, plus Cryo Azacca and Cryo Citra. A high class NEIPA packing superlative tropical fruit flavours and a luxury-style texture.

TOP BUZZER – DDH PALE ALE (SIMCOE STRATA) – 5.6% ABV

Top Buzzer is penthouse-level juice. Simcoe and Strata in peak physical condition with that DDH smoothment. Popping with pineapple and peach at the push of a button.

LONELY TOWN – SESSION IPA (CENTENNIAL IDAHO 7 CITRA) – 4.0% ABV

Feeling like a lonely walker on a lonely street? Take solace in this new Session IPA. Centennial, Idaho 7 and Citra walk hand-in-hand in this resinous, tropical treat.

FAMOUS BLUE CAGOULE – PALE ALE (NELSON CITRA) – 4.0% ABV

Famous Blue Cagoule is our highly practical Pale Ale for uncertain times and unpredictable weather. Citra and Nelson zip across the palate with those upbeat notes of grape and mango, and keep the finish nice and dry.

PARK VIEW – SUMMER WIT (MANDARINA BAVARIA) – 4.5% ABV

Park View, a super-quenching Summer Wit hopped with Mandarina Bavaria. Delicate notes of clementine and lemon in this ultra-pale, spritzy and aromatic Witbier are perfectly paired with sitting on a blanket.

TROPICAL DELUXE – PALE ALE (CITRA SIMCOE MOSAIC) – 3.8% ABV

Tropical Deluxe is our core pale ale. Citra, Mosaic and Simcoe do the tropical dry hop tango, while the mighty London Ale III yeast strain keeps things soft, light, and as level as a hammock. Lower in alcohol, deluxer in tropical.

BASS STRAIT – NEW WORLD HELLES LAGER – 4.7% ABV

Bass Strait is a New World Helles Lager, where the addition of Motueka hops from New Zealand add a gentle, floral fruitiness to the classically bready Helles texture and crisp finish.

27.01 British Culture Archive

Holidays In The Sun // DDH Double IPA

NEW !!!

8.7% ABV // 1.082 OG // 7 EBC // 20 IBU // 440ML

INGREDIENTS: Malted BARLEY (GLUTEN), WHEAT (GLUTEN), OATS (GLUTEN), Dextrose, Hops, Yeast

This DIPA forms the first in a new patrons series in collaboration with the British Culture Archive. The series will be IPA focused, but also intended to showcase some of the progression in the UK hops over the past few years, pairing them with a US variety to show that homegrown varieties can deliver on many of the big, citrus flavours we love from the pacific northwest.

First up we’ve selected Olicana, a variety which has been around for a few years now and still is up there amongst the forefront of UK hops. Olicana stands out for its impressive aromas of mango and passion fruit, backed up by nectarine and apricot notes, and a pleasant floral character. We paired this with one of our current favourite US varieties in Idaho 7, a real allrounder that can be relied upon for plenty of ripe tropical fruit of its own whether used hot side or in the dry hop. In this case, we went with both, as we loaded the whirlpool of this oat-filled NE DIPA with Idaho 7 before drying hopping with both varieties to a combined total of 26g/l.
