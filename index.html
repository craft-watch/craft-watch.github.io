INGREDIENTS: WATER, Malted BARLEY (GLUTEN), WHEAT (GLUTEN) OATS (GLUTEN), HOPS, YEAST

You’ve got to have Faith. Our signature hazy pale ale packed with soft fruit flavours and a citrus hit. Juicy, fruity, fresh.

Now available in mini-cask, ready to be delivered direct to your door, for the ultimate home pub experience.

Please Note: ﻿This product will ship on the 10th - 11th December.

Read on for details of how to store your mini-cask, and how to pour the ultimate pint of Faith.

Make sure to keep your mini cask stored upright and at 8-12 C. It’s especially important to ensure it is stored like this for at least a day before serving. When stored correctly, mini casks will last for 14 days after they’ve been packaged and are best drank within three days of opening.

When you’re ready to pour, twist the handle at the top by 90 degrees. You should hear a hiss. Then, pull the red tape out. The mini cask will be ready to pour, with a little anticlockwise twist. Pop your glass underneath, tilt at an angle, and watch the magic happen. As your glass fills, start to straighten it out again. Make sure to close the tap back by turning clockwise. Close the vent at the top after you’re finished. This’ll help keep the beer fresh for longer.

And there you have it. Fresh Faith.

A Cozy Decomposure

DDH IPA

NEW !!!

A Cozy Decomposure

Range: Augment

Style: DDH India Pale Ale

ABV: 6.6%

Azacca is a big favourite here at Polly’s HQ and for good reason. Named for the Haitian god of agriculture, and bred specifically to accentuate the intense sweet, tropical notes it imparts, we find this varietal grossly underused and underrated in a sea of more popular hype hops. Letting it do its thing in a single hop showcase seemed the most fitting tribute for this awesome hop, and in a “well, why not” move we decided to up the dry-hop bill to DIPA levels, with an insane 40kg added during fermentation.

Wrapped up in a hearty lashing of LA3 for maximum suspension, the result is a thick, juicy IPA, bursting with tonnes of mango, stone fruit, and pineapple notes, before levelling off with a healthy tickling of bitterness on the end for ultimate balance.
