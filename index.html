IPA

NEW !!!

Bonus Tracks

Range: Augment

Style: India Pale Ale

ABV: 6.4%

We’ll always maintain that a Citra and Mosaic base dry hop is unbeatable, but where to go from there? We’re already massive Sabro converts, so it was a given that we’d be adding this, but why not throw some Bru-1 into the mix – a hop varietal we’ve as yet not used at Polly’s. Named for an “ism” coined from the brewery when we discovered all these new varietals now available, we’ve supercharged this beer with a dry hop of 40kg, resulting in freshly cut pineapple aroma and flavours from Bru-1 complimenting the pina colada notes of Sabro, and accentuating the tropical fruit notes of Citra and Mosaic in an IPA that is mega juicy, mega hazy, and mega Polly’s.

Ghost Voices

Range: Augment

Style: Single-Hopped Simcoe DIPA

ABV: 8.2%

The best things really do happen in trilogies – Star Wars, The Godfather, Back to the Future; we could go on. We’ve gone there with Mosaic, we’ve gone there with Citra, and now it’s the turn of probably the biggest in-house favourite hop varietal here at Polly’s; Simcoe. Ghost Voices is the final entry of our exploration into single hopped DIPAs fermented out with everybody’s new favourite super-yeast, Kveik. Tangerines and candied oranges on the nose, follow up with a full, thick, pulpy mouthfeel; bursting with all those incredible passionfruit, mango and berry flavours, whilst retaining just a little bit of earthy bitterness to keep everything in line.

Imperial Stout *NEW* 4 Pack

NEW !!!Mixed case

This 4 pack of beer includes 2 of our most ambitious and biggest stout releases of late.

2 x My Continuous Improvement #3 - Blueberry & Maple Syrup, 11% -Picture a fat stack of thick, chocolate chip pancakes topped with a mound of blueberries and smothered in maple syrup. Or a huge wedge of blueberry pie that's been drizzled with dark chocolate sauce, as if it wasn't indulgent enough already. We've built on our My Continuous Improvement base with bold additions of blueberry and maple syrup to create a smooth, luscious Imperial Stout that balances juicy berry flavours with rich dark chocolate and sweet maple. (440ml)

2 x Black Is Beautiful, 10% - Spiced Imperial Stout made in collaboration with Rock Leopard. This Imperial Stout is our contribution to the Black Is Beautiful initiative hosted by Weathered Souls Brewing Co and has been brewed with our friends from Rock Leopard. It's built on the rich, decadent base from My Continuous Improvement with a complex spice profile that includes African cinchona bark, orange peel, cloves, cinnamon sticks, dandelion root, kaffir lime leaves, cardamom pods, tonka beans, birch leaves, star anise and caraway seeds. The Black is Beautiful initiative is a collaborative effort amongst the brewing community and its customers, in an attempt to bring awareness to the injustices that many people of colour face daily. Its mission is to bridge the gap that's been around for ages and provide a platform to show that the brewing community is an inclusive place for everyone of any colour. 100% of all proceeds (not just profits) from this beer have been donated to three organisations fighting the injustices, crimes, hostility and brutality facing Black people in our country. These organisations are Kids Of Colour, Rainbow Noir, and The Racial Justice Network. (440ml)
